短视频平台教育广告研究——基于74则教育广告的内容分析


李嘉豪  古新语  马瑞  梁燕平

采用内容分析法,以5家在线教育机构发布在抖音、快手平台上的74则短视频教育广告为研究对象,从外在特征和内在特征两个方面对教育广告进行分析。外在特征包括教育广告涉及学科、学段、呈现方式、人物着装、视频时长,内在特征包括广告诉求和所蕴含的价值观念。研究发现,速成学习理念、教育娱乐化导向、应试教育理念、教育焦虑情绪是教育广告最主要的宣传点。透过教育广告可以看出在线教育行业的乱象。为促进在线教育行业健康发展,要制定教育广告专项法规,规范教育广告内容;要确保教育"在线",发挥在线教育机构的教育性;更需要教育机构"修炼内功",在课程质量、师资力量上下真功夫。

Science and Technology Teacher Education in the Anthropocene:Addressing Challenges in the South and North


Peterliang  CoderPig  Mike  Jack

This unique book compares anthropogenic challenges in science and technology teacher education between the southern context of South Africa and the northern context of Sweden. Presenting the results of a three-year research collaboration between science and technology teacher education researchers from Sweden and South Africa, the book explores theoretical perspectives and pedagogical experiences in response to challenges in the Anthropocene. It discusses research-informed practice in teacher education to address sustainable development. Chapters in the book collectively investigate the influence of current environmental and societal changes on the education of teachers, answering the question of how science and technology teacher education can adjust.
